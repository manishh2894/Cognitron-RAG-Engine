Company Leave Policy

All full-time employees are entitled to 21 paid vacation days per year. Sick leave is unlimited but requires manager approval for periods longer than 3 consecutive days.

Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with prior approval from their direct supervisor. All remote work days must be scheduled at least 48 hours in advance.

IT Support

For technical issues, contact the IT helpdesk at it-support@company.com or call extension 1234. Emergency IT support is available 24/7 for critical systems.

Office Hours

Standard office hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. The office is closed on weekends and public holidays.
